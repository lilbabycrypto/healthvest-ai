# TIPS FOR VEO 3.1

1. **Be specific** - Include details about lighting, camera movement, colors
2. **Mention style** - "cinematic", "documentary", "modern", "clean"
3. **Specify duration** - Most shots work best at 5-10 seconds
4. **Iterate** - Generate multiple versions and pick the best
5. **Keep it simple** - Veo works best with clear, single-concept shots
